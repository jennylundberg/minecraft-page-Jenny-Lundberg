MINECRAFT FOR BEGINNERS

Minecraft is a great game for everyone old, teenagers and kids.

Basic tips

1. CHOP DOWN A TREE AND GATHER WOOL

The wood and wool allow you to make a bed which acts as a future spawn point as well as a way to avoid the night creatures in the beginning of the game.

3. CRAFTING TABLES AND FURNACES

In order to make almost anything, you need a crafting table. Once created you can make everything from a rudimentary wooden axe to fully powered train track. However, in order to cook food or smellt ore into usable materials you will need a furnace and something to burn.

2. YOUR FIRST HOME

Before the sun sets on the first day try and get a small house built (about 5Ã—5 is fine). This can be done with dirt of 2 blocks hight. Seal yourself in on the first night as you can always dig you way out in the morning.

4. TORCHES ARE YOUR BEST FRIEND

Make sure you also have a good supply of torches. When mining they are invaluable to light your mines and when you discover caverns they are required to light them up. Many mines already have torches lit but there are many areas still in the dark.

Settings

When you create a world you can use diffrent settings, such as survival, creative, being able to do both and change in between. You can also change biomes, having a spawnchest and much more.
